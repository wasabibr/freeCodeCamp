François Viète

A man in love with Algebra

Here's a time line of Viète's life:

  • 1540 - Born at Fontenay-le-Comte in present-day Vendée, France
  • 1559 - Graduating as a Bachelor of Laws at Poitiers, France
  • 1573 - Became a councillor of the Parliament of Brittany, at Rennes
  • 1571 - Published "Canon mathématique"
  • 1572 - Was in Paris during the St. Bartholomew's Day massacre
  • 1573 - Became a councillor of the Parliament of Brittany, at Rennes
  • 1589 - Published "Deschiffrement d'une lettre escripte par le Commandeur Moreo au Roy d'Espaigne son maître"
  • 1591 - Published "In artem analyticem isagoge"
  • 1591 - Published "Zeteticorum libri quinque"
  • 1593 - Published "Effectionum geometricarum canonica recensio"
  • 1593 - Published "Supplementum geometriae"
  • 1593 - Published "Variorum de rebus mathematicis responsorum liber VIII"
  • 1595 - Published "Ad problema quod omnibus mathematicis totius orbis construendum proposuit Adrianus Romanus, Francisci Vietae responsum"
  • 1598 - Was granted special leave. Henry IV, however, charged him to end the revolt of the Notaries, whom the King had ordered to pay back their fees. Sick and exhausted by work, he left the King's service in December 1602 and received 20,000 écu, which were found at his bedside after his death.
  • 1600 - Accused Clavius, in a series of pamphlets, of introducing corrections and intermediate days in an arbitrary manner, and misunderstanding the meaning of the works of his predecessor, particularly in the calculation of the lunar cycle.
  • 1600 - Published "De numerosa potestatum ad exegesim resolutione"
  • 1600 - Published "Apollonius Gallus"
  • 1602 - Published "Fontenaeensis libellorum supplicum in Regia magistri relatio Kalendarii vere Gregoriani ad ecclesiasticos doctores exhibita Pontifici Maximi Clementi VIII"
  • 1603 - Died on 23 February
  • 1612 - Published "Supplementum Apollonii Galli"
  • 1612 - Published "Supplementum Apollonii Redivivi sive analysis problematis bactenus desiderati ad Apollonii Pergaei doctrinam a Marino Ghetaldo Patritio Regusino hujusque non ita pridem institutam"
  • 1615 - Published "Ad Angularum Sectionem Analytica Theoremata F. Vieta primum excogitata at absque ulla demonstratione ad nos transmissa, iam tandem demonstrationibus confirmata"
  • 1615 - Published "Pro Zetetico Apolloniani problematis a se jam pridem edito in supplemento Apollonii Redivivi Zetetico Apolloniani problematis a se jam pridem edito; in qua ad ea quae obiter inibi perstrinxit Ghetaldus respondetur"
  • 1615 - Published "Francisci Vietae Fontenaeensis, De æquationum — recognitione et emendatione tractatus duo per Alexandrum Andersonum"
  • 1617 - Published "Animadversionis in Franciscum Vietam, a Clemente Cyriaco nuper editae brevis diakrisis"
  • 1619 - Published "Exercitationum Mathematicarum Decas Prima"
